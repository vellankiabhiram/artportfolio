Accomplishments

  • VASE (Visual Art Scholastic Event) State 2x
  • UIL 6A State Traditional Animation Second Place Ranking (Awarded for 2D animated film, “Finder”, a fish dating commercial parody of Tinder.)

  • All American High School Film Festival 2025 (“5 Stages of Sandwich” admitted as Official Selection in Animation Category)
